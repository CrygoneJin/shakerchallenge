# BACKLOG.md — Product Backlog

**Owner:** Leader (/leader)
**Commitment:** Product Goal

---

## Product Goal

> Give every sales rep a safe place to practise real conversations and come out
> better on the other side — faster than any manager could coach them.

---

## Backlog

Items are ordered by priority. Top items are sprint-ready (fully defined).
Lower items are intentionally rough — they get refined as they rise.

## Refinement notes

- Stories in 🔴 must have acceptance criteria before entering a sprint
- Size: S = 1–2 days, M = 3–5 days, L = 1–2 weeks
- Scientist adds eval acceptance criteria to any story involving scoring
- Designer adds UI acceptance criteria to any story involving screens
- Engineer adds technical acceptance criteria to all stories before sprint start
